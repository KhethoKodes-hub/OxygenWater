Our Story

From a vision of pure hydration to a reality of luxury wellness

Redefining Hydration

OX Water was born from a simple yet powerful idea: that hydration should be more than just quenching thirst—it should be an experience that nourishes both body and soul. Founded in 2020, our journey began with a commitment to create the world's finest oxygen-infused water, combining purity, health benefits, and luxury in every bottle.

Our founders, passionate about wellness and sustainability, recognized a gap in the market for a premium water brand that didn't compromise on either health benefits or environmental responsibility. They envisioned a product that would elevate the everyday act of drinking water into a moment of indulgence and self-care.

Today, OX Water stands as a testament to that vision—a brand that has redefined what it means to hydrate, offering an unparalleled experience that merges cutting-edge science with timeless elegance.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Protecting our planet while delivering premium hydration

Sustainable by Design

At OX Water, we believe that luxury and sustainability are not mutually exclusive. Our commitment to environmental stewardship is woven into every aspect of our business, from sourcing to packaging.

We've pioneered innovative approaches to reduce our carbon footprint, including our state-of-the-art bottling facility that runs on 100% renewable energy and our closed-loop water recycling system that minimizes waste.

Our signature glass bottles are not only elegant but also 100% recyclable, and we've partnered with reforestation organizations to plant a tree for every case of OX Water sold.
