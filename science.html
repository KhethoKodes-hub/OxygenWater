Science FAQs

Answers to common scientific questions

How does the body absorb oxygen from water? +

When you drink oxygen-enriched water, the dissolved oxygen is absorbed through the gastrointestinal tract and enters the bloodstream. This supplemental oxygen can help maintain optimal blood oxygen saturation levels, especially during physical exertion or in low-oxygen environments.

Is oxygen-enriched water safe for daily consumption? +

Yes, OX Water is completely safe for daily consumption. The oxygen levels in our water are within physiological ranges that the human body can comfortably process. We adhere to strict quality control standards and our product has been thoroughly tested for safety and efficacy.

How long does the oxygen stay in the water? +

Our proprietary bottling process ensures that oxygen remains dissolved in the water for an extended period. When stored properly in sealed containers, OX Water maintains its elevated oxygen content for up to 12 months. We recommend consuming within 6 months for optimal benefits.

Can oxygen-enriched water help with altitude sickness? +

While OX Water is not a medical treatment, some studies suggest that oxygen-enriched beverages may help mitigate symptoms of mild altitude sickness by providing supplemental oxygen. However, individuals with severe altitude sickness should seek proper medical attention.
