Frequently Asked Questions

Where is OX Water sourced from? +

OX Water is sourced from the purest natural springs and undergoes an advanced filtration process to ensure the highest quality and purity.

How is OX Water different from regular bottled water? +

OX Water is infused with pure oxygen and essential minerals, providing enhanced hydration benefits. Our water is packaged in premium glass bottles and focuses on both luxury experience and health benefits.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders? +

Yes, we offer wholesale and bulk ordering options for businesses, events, and organizations. Please contact our sales team for pricing and availability.

What are the health benefits of oxygen-infused water? +

OX Water provides enhanced hydration, improved post-exercise recovery, and supports the body's natural detoxification processes. The oxygen infusion helps with temperature regulation, blood pressure maintenance, and proper brain function.

Is OX Water environmentally friendly? +

Absolutely. We're committed to sustainability through our recyclable glass bottles, eco-conscious sourcing methods, and environmentally responsible business practices.
