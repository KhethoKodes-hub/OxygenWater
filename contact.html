Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about OX Water

Where is OX Water sourced from? +

OX Water is sourced from the purest natural springs and undergoes an advanced filtration process to ensure the highest quality and purity. Our water is then enriched with oxygen using proprietary technology.

How is OX Water different from regular bottled water? +

OX Water is infused with pure oxygen and essential minerals, providing enhanced hydration benefits. Our water is packaged in premium glass bottles and focuses on both luxury experience and health benefits. The oxygen infusion supports better cellular hydration and overall wellness.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders? +

Yes, we offer wholesale and bulk ordering options for businesses, events, and organizations. Please contact our sales team for pricing and availability. We provide customized solutions for hotels, restaurants, gyms, and corporate clients.

What are the health benefits of oxygen-infused water? +

OX Water provides enhanced hydration, improved post-exercise recovery, and supports the body's natural detoxification processes. The oxygen infusion helps with temperature regulation, blood pressure maintenance, and proper brain function. Many users report increased energy levels and mental clarity.

Is OX Water environmentally friendly? +

Absolutely. We're committed to sustainability through our recyclable glass bottles, eco-conscious sourcing methods, and environmentally responsible business practices. We're proud to be a carbon-neutral company and actively participate in water conservation initiatives.

How long does the oxygen stay in the water? +

Our proprietary bottling process ensures that oxygen remains dissolved in the water for an extended period. When stored properly, OX Water maintains its oxygen content for up to 12 months. We recommend consuming within 6 months for optimal benefits.
